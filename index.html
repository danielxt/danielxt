About Us

The Assembly Soup Kitchen ( T.A.S.K ) started with a handful of volunteers cooking food from their own kitchens - doing what they could with what they had. We are an independent, all-volunteer organisation that serves the homeless and hungry predominately in the Klang Valley. We have been relying on volunteers, local businesses, and the generosity of the community to help us continue our mission of providing hot, nutritious meals to those in need.

As of today, by God’s grace we have had the opportunity to partner with over 200 volunteers, numerous food organisations and are able to provide over 3000 meals a week for the communities in need.

How You Can Help

Volunteer

Volunteering with us is as simple as ABC, and in our case, D! You don’t have to commit to a regular schedule and you can do so in several ways. Find out which suits you and get in touch with us!

Assist in packing

We have volunteers on some days at our HQ to help us with packing. We put together care packs and packed meals before distributing them to our beneficiaries.

Bake from home

We receive baking ingredients as donations and our volunteers bring them home to bake in their own time. Baked goods are sent to our HQ to be distributed.

Cook from home

A weekly occasion, and a highlight for many. Volunteers register their interest on Wednesdays, collect cooking ingredients following Friday afternoon and cook a storm on Saturdays in their own kitchen. Because of them, we get to bless our beneficiaries with home cooked meals every Saturday.

Distribute

At TASK, we go to our communities. We need volunteers to join us a few days a week delivering care packs and meals to our beneficiaries. We observe COVID-19 precautionary measures during our delivery and equip our volunteers with PPE.

Visit our Facebook and Instagram pages to see our volunteers in action! Send us a private message to get involved.

Non-monetary

We very much welcome non-monetary contribution in the form of dried goods, face masks, frozen food and fresh vegetables. However, our needs would very much depend on the requests of our beneficiaries and it may differ week-to-week. Please contact us through Facebook or Instagram to check with us on our needs prior to making your contribution.
