About Us

The Assembly Soup Kitchen ( T.A.S.K ) started with a handful of volunteers cooking a small amount of food from their own kitchens - doing what we can with what we had. We are an independent, all-volunteer organisation that serves the homeless and hungry predominately in the Klang Valley. We have been relying on volunteers, local businesses, and the generosity of the community to help us continue our mission of providing hot, nutritious meals to those in need.

As of today, by God’s grace we have had the opportunity to partner with over 200 volunteers, numerous food organisations and are able to provide over 3000 meals a week for the communities in need.
